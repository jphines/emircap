ABOUT EMRICAP

Emricap is an incubator company in the Ft. Lauderdale area that is focused on developing business ventures between India and the United States. We provide angel investment, start-up management, network development, and venture-capital search assistance to entrepreneurs. We serve start-up companies geared at marketing goods and services to the growing Indian middle class. In addition, we invest in Indian goods and services marketed to US businesses and consumers.
